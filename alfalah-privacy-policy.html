Privacy Policy

At Al Falah Educational Centre, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in accordance with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you submit forms or interact with our services:

  • Student details (name, date of birth, gender, school year, program interest)
  • Parent/guardian details (name, phone number, email, relationship to student)
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • Processing admissions applications
  • Communicating important updates about programs and services
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Protection

We will never sell or rent your personal information. Data may be shared with authorized personnel for administrative purposes only. We implement strict technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectify inaccurate information
  • Right to request erasure of your data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@alfalah.edu.

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies on our website to enhance user experience. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies; however, some features of the site may not function properly without them.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
