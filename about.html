Our History & Journey

Al Falah Educational Centre was established in 1998 with a vision to provide quality Islamic education to the Muslim community in Harrow and surrounding areas.

What began as a small weekend Madrasah with just 50 students has grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving over 800 students across multiple programs. Our journey has been guided by the principle of combining traditional Islamic knowledge with contemporary educational methods.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities, developed specialized curricula, and trained dedicated teachers to ensure we provide the highest standard of Islamic education while maintaining the warmth and closeness of a community madrasah.

Our Vision

Shaping Future Generations

To establish a safe and inspiring learning environment where students can develop strong Islamic identities, connect with the Qur'an, and build character that will guide them throughout their lives.

We envision generations of confident Muslims who are proud of their faith, contribute positively to society, and serve as ambassadors of Islamic values in their communities.

Our Educational Approach

Qur'an First

The Holy Qur'an is at the heart of everything we do. Students begin with proper Tajweed and progress to memorization and understanding.

Character Building

We focus on developing Islamic character (Akhlaq) through practical examples from the Seerah and daily application.

Community Focus

Education extends beyond the classroom. We foster a sense of community and brotherhood among students and families.
